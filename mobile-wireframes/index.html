Design Thinking Process

1. Empathize

User research identified that people want to help the environment but don't know where to start. We defined two key personas: the "Urban Minimalist" and the "Suburban Saver".

2. Define

The core problem: Sustainability tracking feels like a chore. The solution must be low-friction and visually rewarding.
